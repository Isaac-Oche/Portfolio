Practice

Harbor Studio prefers fewer moves, executed with fidelity. Drawing sets stay legible for builders; interiors trust plaster, oak, honed stone, steel in thin gauges. Landscapes extend rooms rather than framing them like pictures.

The studio maintains a deliberately small cohort so principals stay hands-on from concept sketches through site walks and punch lists.
